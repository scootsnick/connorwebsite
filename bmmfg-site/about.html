About Us

We are a small, local distribution company based in Paterson, NJ, dedicated to serving the needs of mom-and-pop shops, corner stores, and bodegas in our community. Our focus is on providing grocery and household essentials that keep neighborhood businesses stocked with the products their customers rely on every day. Because we’re local, we understand the unique needs of smaller retailers and are able to offer a more personal, reliable, and flexible service than larger distributors.

While our core business is groceries and household goods, we also distribute tools and select home improvement items, giving our partners access to a wider range of products without the need to juggle multiple suppliers. Our mission is to help small businesses thrive by delivering quality products at fair prices and building long-term relationships rooted in trust, consistency, and community support.
